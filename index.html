principal tone ornament playing
The system: a division splits one or more octaves into equal parts — the principal tones. Each principal carries ornaments: interpolation passes inside the gap, infrapolation dips below, ultrapolation overshoots the next principal before landing. Combined forms play their ornaments in the order named. The chord menu lists what this family actually outlines — pick a sound, get the patterns that spell it.
Keys: Space play/stop · ↑↓ pattern · ←→ root · L loop · D drone · R dice. With loop on, keys takes the pattern up half-steps or around the cycle of fourths automatically.
